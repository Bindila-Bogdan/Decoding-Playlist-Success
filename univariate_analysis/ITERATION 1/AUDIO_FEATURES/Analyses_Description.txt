1. Song Level Audio Features:
   Description: Analyze individual audio features like danceability, energy, etc., for songs.
   Method: Compute summary statistics for each feature.
   Visualization: Box plots or histograms displaying the distribution of each audio feature.

2. Track Duration Analysis:
   Description: Understand the distribution of track lengths.
   Method: Calculate statistical measures (e.g., average, median, range) for the duration of tracks.
   Visualization: Histograms or box plots illustrating the variation in track lengths.

3. Mode of Tracks:
   Description: Analyze the distribution of the mode field to determine the prevalence of major versus minor keys in tracks.
   Method: Calculate the frequency or proportion of tracks in major and minor keys.
   Visualization: Bar chart or pie chart showing the distribution of track modes (major vs. minor).
